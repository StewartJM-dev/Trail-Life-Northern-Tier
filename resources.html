Troop Fundraising Resources

Don't let limited funds prevent your troop from delivering on the promise of adventure! These proven fundraising solutions help troops across the Northern Tier fund their entire annual program with just one major fundraiser.

Fundraising Best Practices

✓ Plan Your Program First

Hold an annual planning session with parents, leaders, and boys. Create a full calendar of activities, then budget for it.

✓ One Major Fundraiser

Choose ONE fundraiser that covers most/all annual costs. Work together as a team rather than nickle-and-diming families.

✓ 4-5 Days, Not Every Weekend

Dedicate a few focused weeks once per year instead of constant car washes and bake sales.

Trail Life USA Fundraising Policy

All fundraising activities must comply with Trail Life USA's official guidelines:

Required Approvals

  • All fundraisers must be approved by your Chartering Organization
  • Must align with Trail Life USA's motto "Walk Worthy!", Mission, and Trailmen Oath
  • Use of Trail Life USA logo requires approval from Home Office (email Branding@TrailLifeUSA.com at least 3 weeks in advance)

Key Guidelines

  • Maximum 3 fundraisers per year per troop
  • Voluntary participation - All families must be informed; participation is optional
  • Two-deep leadership required at all fundraising activities
  • No solicitation of donations
  • Approved fundraisers may be done in official Trail Life USA uniform
  • Must comply with all Health & Safety guidelines

Prohibited Activities

  • Games of chance, lotteries, or bingo (unless specifically donated and compliant with local laws)
  • Activities that could be construed as gambling
  • Raffle ticket sales (only if consistent with Charter Organization's faith and compliant with all laws)

Need to Submit a Fundraiser?

Download the TLUSA License Application from Trail Life USA's website

Include: Troop number, contact info, duration, product description, costs, and sale price

Email to: Branding@TrailLifeUSA.com

Tips for Fundraising Success

📋 Plan Together

Involve parents in annual planning so they understand and support the need for fundraising.

💰 Show the Budget

Help families see the full cost of the program you're planning. Transparency builds buy-in.

🤝 Work as a Team

Everyone contributes to ONE major fundraiser vs. individual families bearing the burden.

⏰ Focus & Finish

Dedicate 4-5 days over a few weeks. Get it done, then enjoy your fully-funded year!
