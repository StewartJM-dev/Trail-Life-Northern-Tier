Setup Instructions

Follow these steps to set up your photo gallery:

  1. Go to Google Photos (photos.google.com)
  2. Create a new album (click + New Album)
  3. Name it something like "Trail Life Northern Tier Gallery"
  4. Add photos to the album
  5. Click the 3 dots menu â†’ Share

  10. Replace YOUR_GOOGLE_PHOTOS_ALBUM_URL_HERE with your album link
  11. Save and upload to GitHub
