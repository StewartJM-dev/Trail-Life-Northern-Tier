Troop PA-1997

Location: Sweet Valley, PA

Sponsor: Living Word Baptist Church

Chartered: July 2024

Meetings: Every Tuesday at 6:00 PM

Christ-centered outdoor adventure program featuring camping, hiking, archery, rifle shooting, rope work, and biblical character development. Located near Ricketts Glen State Park!

Troop NY-2911

Location: Henrietta, NY

Sponsor: Harvest Bible Fellowship

Address: 1125 Calkins Road, Henrietta NY, 14623

Meetings: Mondays at 6:30 PM (Sep-May main program, Navigators & Adventurers year-round)

Comprehensive program for boys ages 5-17 including archery, knot tying, woods tools, firebuilding, cooking, hiking, orienteering, wilderness survival, canoeing, and more. Programs divided by age groups from Foxes (5-6) through Adventurers (14-17).
