How to Upload Photos:
  1. Go to imgbb.com
  2. Upload your photo (drag & drop)
  3. Click "Embed codes" icon
  4. Select "Direct link" from dropdown
  5. Copy the URL (ends with .jpg or .png)
  6. Paste in Google Sheet image_url column
