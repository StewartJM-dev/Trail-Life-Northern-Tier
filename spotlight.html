October 2025: Troop PA-1997 - Sweet Valley

This month we're spotlighting Troop PA-1997, chartered in July 2024 and sponsored by Living Word Baptist Church in Sweet Valley, Pennsylvania. What began as a dream and a prayer in December 2023 has grown into a thriving brotherhood of young men learning to "Walk Worthy" through outdoor adventure, character development, and biblical discipleship.

Key Highlights:

  • Built a 30-foot flagpole as a troop project
  • Launched model rockets with hands-on STEM learning
  • Conquered trails at Ricketts Glen State Park
  • Weekly meetings focusing on biblical character development
  • Activities include camping, hiking, archery, rifle shooting, rope work, and campfire cooking
  • Located just minutes from Benton and Ricketts Glen State Park
  • Open registration - no waiting periods, join anytime!

"Join us for faith-based outdoor adventures, character development, and lifelong brotherhood. We meet every Tuesday at 6:00 PM at Living Word Baptist Church, 40 Hess Lane, Sweet Valley, PA. No appointment needed!"
